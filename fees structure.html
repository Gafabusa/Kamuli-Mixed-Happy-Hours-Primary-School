Our Programs

At Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours P/S, we offer comprehensive education, focusing on core subjects, critical thinking, extracurricular activities, and technology integration to prepare students for future success.

Religious Affiliation.

We nurture our pupils to fear God, as it is biblical that the fear of the lord is the beginning of wisdom (Proverbs 1:7).

Extracurricular Activities

We promote well-rounded development through sports like football and netball, creative expression in arts and music, and diverse clubs nurturing special interests and talents.

Special Programs

We provide tailored support with remedial classes, enrichment for gifted students, and counseling services to support emotional well-being and academic success.

Community Engagement and Service Learning

We foster community engagement through service projects, environmental education emphasizing sustainability and doing community outreaches.

Academics.

we have a well qualified staff with experience to equip our learners with the best academic knowledge. We also have a fully stocked library to support learners to read more and make research.

Health and Wellness

We promote health with education on nutrition and hygiene, regular physical activity for fitness, and nutritious meals supporting students' overall well-being.
