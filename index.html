About Us

Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours Primary School is a private mixed school. It is a Christian founded school bracing children of every faith. Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours also has a Nursery wing that is fully equipped and giving excellent services. The school is located in Kamuli Municipality, Kamuli District along Brewer Road, Bunangwe zone. The school has been leading the district in PLE for the last ten years.

What We Offer

Discover what makes Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours P/S unique, from scholarships to community engagement and individualized learning.

Bursaries.
At Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours P/S, we believe in the potential of every child and offerbursaries to intellectually capable students lacking financial backing. Our bursaries program focuses on providing merit-based free education to deserving students, ensuring they have the opportunity to succeed.
Community Engagement
Located in Kamuli Municipality, Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours P/S is deeply rooted in the community, engaging with local families, particularly low-income farmers, to promote the importance of quality education. We engage with organizations to enhance our pupil's educational journey.
Why Choose Us?
We offer individualized learning tailored to each pupil's unique needs, delivered by experienced teachers committed to excellence. Our safe, nurturing environment allows pupils to focus on their studies, while our holistic approach ensures their overall development for all aspects of life.
