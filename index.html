About Us

Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours Primary School is a private mixed school.
It is a Christian founded school bracing children of every faith.
Kamuli Mixed Happy Hours also has a Nursery wing that is fully equipped and giving excellent services.
The school is located in Kamuli Municipality, Kamuli District along Brewer Road, Bunangwe zone.
The school has been leading the district in PLE for the last ten years.
